Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Speed is not displaying

Location permission is required. Go to Settings → Speedmeter → Location and select "Always" or "While Using". Also, please use the app outdoors where GPS signal is available.

Q: How accurate is the speed?

Accuracy depends on GPS signal conditions, typically ±2-3 km/h. Accuracy may decrease in areas with weak GPS signal such as between tall buildings or in tunnels.

Q: Does it work in background?

Yes. Set location permission to "Always" and the app will continue recording location in the background.

Q: Battery consumption concerns

GPS usage increases battery consumption. For extended use, we recommend using a car charger.

Q: How to cancel Premium subscription?

Go to Settings app → Apple ID → Subscriptions to cancel. Premium features remain available until the end of the billing period.

Q: How to delete history?

Go to Settings tab in the app → Delete History to clear all history.
