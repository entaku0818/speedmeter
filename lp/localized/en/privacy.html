Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Speedmeter ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information:

  • Location Data: We use GPS to obtain location data for speed measurement and trip history recording.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics through Firebase Analytics to improve the app.
  • Crash Reports: We collect crash information through Firebase Crashlytics to improve app quality.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing speed display and trip history features
  • Improving app functionality and fixing bugs
  • Enhancing user experience

4. Data Storage

Location history data is stored only on your device and is not sent to external servers. Analytics and crash reports are stored anonymously on Google servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Ad delivery (free version only)
  • Firebase Analytics: Usage statistics collection
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash report collection
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Revoke location permission at any time
  • Delete trip history from within the app
  • Opt out of personalized ads
