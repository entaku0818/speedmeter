A simple and easy-to-read GPS speedometer app.

Check your current speed in real-time with a large digital display. Perfect for driving, cycling, running, and more.

Features:
- Real-time speed display (km/h)
- Large, easy-to-read digital display
- 3 font styles (System, Digital Modern, Digital Classic)
- Location history recording with map view
- Color-coded pins based on speed (green: slow, yellow: medium, orange: fast, red: very fast)
- Background recording support

Highlights:
- Simple and intuitive interface
- Dark mode optimized black background design
- Stores up to 1000 location records
- View your route on Apple Maps

Whether you're driving, cycling, or running, this speedometer app is your perfect companion for tracking your speed on the go.
